Gnome More War


Genre: Arcade/Shooter
Platforms:
  • GameJolt
  • Android (2016)
  • iOS (2016)

Description: Gnome More War is a stationary shooter where your goal is to stop the gnomes from stealing from your barn by shooting them down as they parachute down from the opposite cliff. There are power ups to pick up to aid you on your cause. Of course, it won't be so simple as some of these gnomes actually fight back and will try to bring you down, so not only do you have to protect your barn, you have to protect yourself as well.
