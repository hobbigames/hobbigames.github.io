Description



Super Tiny Monkey is an endless platformer with quests, obstacles to avoid, power ups to learn and upgrade, and bosses to fight. You play as Goggles, the experimental tarsier who managed to escape from Dr. Zoo’s laboratory and your goal is to run away from his poachers and minions who are sent to capture you. You must leap, glide, dash, or dive from one platform to the next to get as far away as possible. You must also avoid obstacles and traps, fend off enemies, and fight big bad bosses-- all while trying not to fall to your doom. The game integrates cool RPG elements such as gaining experiencing points, levelling up your character, quests, learning and upgrading special abilities. You can finish the game’s story by completing all Acts as they become available, or just play indefinitely on endless maps and compete with your friends in the game’s leaderboard.


Features


Endless Platforming

Super Tiny Monkey is a platformer and endless runner hybrid, an endless platformer, if you will. It’s an endless runner, because your character is in an infinite state of movement across the level. It’s a platformer because instead of running, your character will be moving from one platform to the next. You control the titular character and you must use precise timing on your input so the character can land safely on the next platform. Also, obstacles such as spikes, creatures, and poachers will try to block your path and bring you down!


Boss Battles

Unlike most runners, Super Tiny Monkey has boss battles. Play enough adventure map so you can stock up on golden bugs and unleash a barrage of power ups against the enemy boss. Perform combos and complete the side missions to earn bonus points.


RPG Elements

Did we mention power ups? Super Tiny Monkey also has a bit of RPG in it. Your character can learn abilities as he gains experience and levels. You can upgrade these abilities and build a strong and powerful character that can blast through the levels and stand toe to toe against the enemy bosses. Another RPG element that can be found in the game is the Quest system. You may complete optional quests to earn golden bugs, experience points, and even rainbow butterflies.


Story Acts

You can play through the story by finishing all Acts. Each act has one adventure map and one boss map. You can also play the adventure maps indefinitely and compete with your friends on the game’s online leaderboard.


About Hobbi Games


Hobbi Games is an independent Philippines based duo of husband and wife game developers who wish to create games that people would love and enjoy. We are both video game enthusiasts, with the right amount of balance between hardcore and casual tastes. Despite this difference in gaming preference, we share a common passion and love for video games, and making games.

Since we’re only a duo, Hobbi Games is also collaborative in nature. It is not uncommon for us to seek help from friends who are also into gaming to give a hand in any aspect of game development. We want to grow bigger of course, that is why we will keep on delivering fun, enjoyable, and quality games until we become a proper game development studio.

Super Tiny Monkey is the third game under our belt, and is more ambitious than the two games before it. We really hope people will enjoy the game!
